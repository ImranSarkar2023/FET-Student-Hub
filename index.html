FET Student Hub is a multi-page website developed for Food Engineering & Technology students.

It serves as a centralized platform providing department notices, academic resources, faculty and student information, engineering utilities such as CGPA and unit converters, interactive calculators, lab records, feedback forms, and other useful student services to support academic and departmental activities.
